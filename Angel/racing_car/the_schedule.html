    • The 2018 Finali Mondiali at Monza Circuit from 1 to 4 November, will give all fans a chance to enjoy four days of excitement and spectacle, marked by Ferrari's unique and exclusive style.

    This event mixes sport, passion, and competition with the unmistakable magic of the historic Formula 1 cars and the incredible power and beauty of the FXX K Evo, the latest car of the exclusive XX Programmes that made its debut at Mugello in 2017.

    The final races of the season in the Ferrari Challenge Trofeo Pirelli Europe, North America and Asia-Pacific championships will take place. On Sunday, a record number of 488 Challenge will compete for the coveted world championship title.

    There is also great excitement ahead of the Ferrari Show on Sunday, which will feature the Scuderia Ferrari demonstrations of pit stops, thrilling accelerations and fast laps on the fastest track of the championship. Ferrari GT racing cars will take a bow at the end of a season in which they competed in the most prestigious series. Starring in Monza will be also the official GT drivers of the Prancing Horse. Aces like Alessandro Pier Guidi and James Calado, reigning champions of the FIA World Endurance Championship, Davide Rigon and Sam Bird, Miguel Molina, 2018 PWC champion also racing in the European Le Mans Series, together with Giancarlo Fisichella, Olivier Beretta and Andrea Bertolini.

    Finally, the Finali Mondiali will offer many activities in the paddock, including some for the youngest Ferrari fans with the special Paddock Junior. Maranello's entire range will be on display, including the brand new Ferrari Monza SP1 and SP2, as well as the Ferrari 488 Pista Spider, presented only a few weeks ago at the Paris Motor Show.
